Meet the team

Ashwin Subramanian

Ashwin is a 16 year old from Bangalore who co-founded UNLOCK because he felt the need to normalise and educate adolescents about the highs and lows of human emotion. Ashwin developed this website with the hope that our webinars reach more teens, including those outside Bangalore. When he is not working on UNLOCK, you’ll find Ashwin on the basketball court or playing the Mridangam!
